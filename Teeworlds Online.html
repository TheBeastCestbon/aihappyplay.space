About Teeworlds

What is Teeworlds?

Teeworlds is a free-to-play online multiplayer 2D shooter game that has captivated gamers worldwide since 2007. Developed as an open-source project, it features fast-paced action, unique physics-based gameplay, and a vibrant community of dedicated players.

The game combines retro-style graphics with modern gameplay mechanics, offering intense battles across various game modes including Deathmatch, Team Deathmatch, and Capture the Flag.
